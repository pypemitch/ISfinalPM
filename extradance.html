My Love for Dance

 


My Favorite Styles of Dance:

  1. Ballet
    • This style is my top favorite and is the most challenging to me
    • I feel the most beautiful when I dance ballet
    • One of my dance gifts is having "ballon". This means when I jump, I fly high in the air and land as if I am a feather

  2. Contemporary
    • This style is the easiest to be very expressive
    • I love the interpretation that can be a part of contemporary dance

  3. Ballroom
    • I have been blessed with a natural gift at ballroom dancing
    • I love dancing with a partner because it requires teamwork and flexibility

  4. Hip Hop
    • This style is so fun with a constant emphasis on the down beat of music
    • Hip hop is a very fast dance style, so you need to be agile

  5. Jazz
    • Jazz is a style full of personality
    • You have to be very quick in jazz dance as well as expressive
